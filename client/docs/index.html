Windfish

Windfish is a game tracker that works in all major web browsers and is designed for tracking progress in The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening DX randomizer

Features

Overworld

Keep tabs on places you've been, places you're going and what was found when you got there!

  • 50+ map markers
    • Dungeons, chests, NPCs, entrances and landmarks
    • Entrances are linked together; hovering shows both points
    • Dungeon markers indentify their accessibility based on your current items
  • Multiple overlays including entrances and collectibles
  • Use different map images to ease your navigation

Items

Just activate an item or event and forget about pulling up your menu to check again!

  • Different layout modes to expand on what you want to track
    • Default: Required Items & Dungeons
    • Extended: Collectibles & Keys
    • Super-Extended: Pieces of Heart, Songs & Events
  • Track the amount of potential unique chests in dungeons

Timer

Track your current run with just a click!

  • Timer can start and stop whenever you like
  • Save your best time to compare against later
  • Current time will change color based on approach to previous time

Twitch.tv Integration

Streaming your game? Let your chat help you track your items!

  • Customizable chat commands
  • Commands can activate anything in the Items tracker
  • WindFishBot automatically joins your channel
  • Add/remove users to a Whitelist

Other Features

  • Persistent settings
  • Visual customizations
